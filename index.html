The Profile

I am an independent songwriter and multi-instrumentalist specializing in lyric-driven acoustic music centered on themes of faith, grace, and redemption. My work seeks to balance the theological weight of historical hymns with the raw, intimate instrumentation of traditional roots and indie-folk.

Based out of the studio, I partner with local communities, independent filmmakers, and publishing labels to deliver authentic pieces that draw listeners into deep contemplation and worship.
