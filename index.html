The Heart Behind the Music

Welcome. I am an independent songwriter exploring the intersection of traditional acoustic folk music and deep, scriptural storytelling. My music is stripped-back, lyric-driven, and focused on the timeless themes of grace, worship, and walking through the wilderness. Every track is an invitation to pause, breathe, and reflect on the Creator.
